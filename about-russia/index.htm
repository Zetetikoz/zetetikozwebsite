Living in Russia is completely a very unique experience. Along with the good education system you will find Russia very systematic and civilized. Russian peoples are well mannered, kind hearted and helpful. It doesn’t matter which race or which country you belong they will welcome you with open heart and respect, which is why most of the students who wants live off campus rent a room in a family apartments. As compare to any other European country Russia is cheaper to live.

GENERAL FACTS

Russia or Russian Federation is a country with great history, home of famous scientists and land of natural beauty.  

Capital: Moscow

President: Vladimir Putin

Russia  covers area of 17 075 500 km2 which makes Russia the largest country in the world.

Since decades Russia was a center of research and education. Russia is the place where maximum number of foreigner students arrive every year for higher education in the fields of medicine, engineering, economics, natural and social sciences.

Russian education system is considered to be the best system in the world. Today Russia has maximum number of scientists in the field of medicine and technology.

Higher Studies in Russia Are affordable unlike other countries.

The global high standard education that Russia lures a lot of foreign students who wish to study MBBs in Russia. Moreover, the low tuition fee and cost of living makes Russia favorite choice of MBBS and degree courses all over the world. 

CLIMATE AND NATURE

The beauty of Russia’s natural landscapes will take your breath away!

All four seasons – summer, autumn, winter and spring – are easily distinguished. Each season lasts for approximately three months, making the climate comfortable. As Russia stretches across a vast area from south to north and from west to east, the climate varies in different parts of the country.
Winter is usually cold In Russia with average temperature of -5 .temperature may fall -15 to -20 for a week or two. Siberian region is a bit more cold than other parts of Russia but it is bearable for all communities of the world as there are lot of foreigners from warm areas of Africa and India and they enjoy this weather. Russia has about 3 million km of rivers. There are many lakes in Russia, fresh- or salt-water. Lake Baikal is the largest fresh-water lake in the world, it has maximum depth of 1620 m. Russian Climate is the world’s best climate for healthy life it is enriched with fresh air and oxygen.

POPULATION AND SOCIETY

Spend an amazing time in surrounds of a friendly and warm-hearted people!

Russia has the world’s fifth largest population (148,8 million people) after China, India, the United States and Indonesia.
All in all, 73 per cent of Russian citizens live in urban areas. Society is open modern and educated. Everyone can live his own life freely and safely. People are cooperative and open hearted. Foreign students get easily communicate with Russians. Russia is having maximum literacy rate all over the world as education is compulsory and free for all citizens. 

SAFETY

Feel safe and calm!

The Russian Federation is a very safe and secure country! Staying in a hostel, walking along the streets, travelling within the country you will not have any worries about something bad happening to you. If you get problems and need someone’s help, for sure you will find people who try to fix your problems or tell you where you should go and how to get somewhere. Campuses, hostels and Universities are equipped by safety cameras and security systems. The Universities’ Security Centers provide 24-hour security cover for the Universities’ campuses and halls of residence. A comprehensive CCTV camera systems operate through the Universities’ campuses, including some of the large residential sites. The cameras are used to direct security staff to incidents, and the Centers work in close liaison with the police. 

CUISINE

Taste the diverse flavors of the Russian cuisine. You won’t be able to resist!

Russian cuisine is distinctive and unique, and as any other national one, it was formed under the influence of different environmental, social, geographical, economic and historical factors. The main feature of Russian cuisine is considered to be an abundance and variety of products used for cooking.  Russian cuisine offers a great number of vegetarian dishes including mushrooms, pickles etc.  The Russian culinary palette is also rich in meat, poultry and fish dishes which are served on various occasions.  

CULTURE AND FOLK ART

You will definitely get inspired by cultural heritage and modern art!

Russia has over 50,000 state public libraries in total possession of over a billion books. There are about 1500 museums (historical, ethnographic. memorial, of folk crafts, fine and applied arts, theatre, music, natural sciences, technology and many others). Twenty open-air ethnographic museums present folk architecture, arts and everyday life.

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGIES

Discover the most up-to-date scientific and technical research and the famous inventions of notable Russian scientists!

Russia was the first one in developing and exploring of many branches of scientific research and achievements: 

Main Inventions: first in the world: light bulb, radio, television, helicopter, space rocket, video recorder, mainline locomotive, color photograph, animated feature, intensifying and generating semiconductor device, building combined machine, grain harvester, model of mechanical heart, thermonuclear and atomic bomb, gasoline-powered engine and many others

In Russia there are about 4,000 organizations involved in scientific research and development. About 70% of these organizations belong to the government.

STUDENT LIFE

Become a student of a prestigious state university and experience independent life full of new impressions, emotions and friends!

Student life…this is not just about study. You will visit museums, cinemas, interesting concerts, shows and performances; you will meet new friends from all parts of our globe. This is a fun, fulfilling, exciting experience.

During the time of study in Russia foreign students do not miss a chance to experience the unique Russian hospitality, the history and the culture of the country reflected in the sacred heritage left for later generations, to be enchanted by the authentic beauty of Russian nature, as well as its cities and towns comprising ancient and modern architectural styles.
