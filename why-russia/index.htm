Since the era of technological and industrial development, Russian higher education is very famous for its superiority within the areas of scientific research. Russian degree holders are in high demand by international companies and they get good jobs very easily in different countries. Russian universities have a quite good network with universities all over the world.

MAIN ADVANTAGES WHY TO CHOOSE RUSSIA?

  • Quality Education with teaching staff more than 90 % consisting of Professors and
    Candidates of Sciences;
  • Medium of Instructions is English during the whole period of study;
  • Easy Admission Process;
  • No entrance exams;
  • Access to the most modern laboratories, technical and research centers;
  • Globally Recognized Courses (Europe, the UK, the USA, Asia, Africa);
  • Medical degree recognized by WHO, UNESCO, PLAB (the UK), USMLE (the USA), MCI, PMDC, other International Organizations and throughout the EU;
  • IELTS/TOEFL or other certificates alike are not required;
  • Worldwide Acceptance of Russian Method of Teaching and Learning;
  • Mess with Indian cooker 3 times per day;
  • Textbooks and studying materials are provided for the students for free;
  • Practical classes in hospital from the 2nd year;
  • Coaching for our students to prepare them for national and internationals licensing
    examination from the 3rd year (MCI, USMLE, etc);
  • Internship in the biggest hospitals of Russia;
  • Emphasis on Practical Aspect in Teaching;
  • One of the Best Transportation systems in the world;
  • 100% guarantee to get an ADMISSION and INVITATION LETTER.
  • 100% visa for deserving students;
  • Best opportunities for clinical exposure at university run hospitals;
  • No Donation;
  • All our universities are Government Universities of Russia;
  • Widest range of educational programs available;
  • Great campuses and facilities offered;
  • Education that combines traditional and modern innovation;
  • Offers great social life for international students.

Russia is a major economic and political player in the world, this is one of various reasons why reputation of universities and the obtained graduate degree are well-known all over the world and show the future employer that the student had a firm basic education and  further knowledge of practical work.

The large focus on research projects is also a reason for the good reputation of Russian universities. During the educational process student to do their own projects and learn their future job in an actual real-life situation. Russia provides you with globally reputed universities and institutes with affordable tuition fee.

Due to the high quality education most of the Russian universities remain at top positions in international rankings for years.
