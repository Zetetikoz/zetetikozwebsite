zetetikoz has the vision of turning your Dream into Reality and for that we aim at reaching out to the aspiring students and provide them with the necessary guidance and support to fulfil their dream to become a professional. To create awareness about the education opportunities available in Abroad.

zetetikoz completely assist our students on each step, including admission process, visa assistance, receiving on arrival, documentation process, personal guidance to each student for the whole duration of study!   

zetetikoz education counsellors guide you through the path of shortlisting the country and university best suiting your career goals as well as budget preferences.

zetetikoz has representatives in each city where our partner Universities are situated to offer better assistance to our Students till their Graduation!

 

WE PROVIDE FOLLOWING MAJOR SERVICES TO OUR STUDENTS:  

  • Instant replies to our students through E-mail or phone;
  • Helping students to choose the right course and right university;
  • Assistance in choosing appropriate Bachelor/Master program;
  • Invitation letter from a certain/recommended University;
  • Provide students with the Admission letter;
  • VISA support letter from the Department of Higher Education in Ukraine/Russia (through Ministry of Education and ministry of Foreign Affairs);
  • Pre-departure guidance;
  • Airport pick up with Zetetikoz representative holding —Official permission and Identity to avoid any unpleasant circumstances for student;
  • Provides a complimentary call from the university to parents
  • Escorting Student to the University;
  • Assistance in Immigration Clearance on Arrival;
  • Accommodation arrangement in hostel;
  • Assistance in Health Insurance;
  • Assistance in Medical check ups on Arrival;
  • Assist you in final documentation in the university
  • Assistance in Registration with the Department of Visa and Registration;
  • Visa Support throughout the study period
  • Student ID card, hostel card, International student card (free of cost);
  • Opening a Bank Account in Ukraine/Russia;
  • Assist you in getting local SIM Card in University
  • Support to our students during the whole period;
  • Arrangement of Separate rooms for female students;
  • Visa support during the entire period of study;
  • Providing assistance in obtaining the Certificate of Equivalence from the Ministry of Education of the Destination Country;
  • Support during the entire period of study;
  • Arranging MCI screening test coaching classes in the university;
  • Orientation program for students in the local environment (city exploration);
  • Any other assistance that may be required by the students.

SERVICE FOR PARENTS:  

  • Obtaining and sending the tourism invitation;
  • Reservation of hostel and other places for our clients;
  • Escorting our clients from airport to hostels;
  • Delivering car and drivers for daily excursion;
  • Providing translator.
