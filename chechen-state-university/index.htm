ABOUT GROZNY

Grozny is a city in Russia, the capital of the Chechnya Republic, standing on both banks of the Sunzha River, the tributary of the Terek River. The population of Grozny is about 283,700 (2015), with an area of – 324 sq. km.

Grozny is replete with many new modern buildings, business centers and new beautiful office buildings. The construction of new facilities does not stop here for a single day. In Grozny, the streets are actively restored, which gives the city a special comfort, which is not found in every city in the North Caucasus. Regardless of whether you were just passing through Grozny, or you came here for a visit, you will immediately feel that you are taking care of, by the inhabitants.

Transportation is available almost around the clock, which can easily take you anywhere in Grozny and beyond.

In Grozny, over the past decade, many new schools and universities have opened. Education in the city is reaching a new level, which attracts young people from all the republics of the North Caucasus to the city.

 

ABOUT CHECHEN STATE UNIVERSITY

Chechen State University opened on 7th of February 1938. Currently around 15000 full-time and part-time students are receiving education at Chechen State University with 10 Faculties and four Institutes leading the learning process. 

University works hard to create an environment where students can not only gain quality education but also develop themselves personally. Student life at our University is filled with exciting events full of creativity and scientific inquiry. 

The University won – third year in a row, the competition- “Development programmes of student communities”, commissioned by the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation. As part of the Programme, the University successfully realised a number of projects and opened new facilities & Projects like – International Youth Centre, School of Student Leadership, Recording Studio, Healthy Lifestyle Centre and Student Evaluation Commission “For Quality Education”. For those keen on sports there are 14 sporting societies, a student sports club, and a swimming pool.

The Education Process incorporates modern technologies. The classrooms are equipped with interactive whiteboards, hardware essential for video conferencing, extensive resources of online-library, both Russian and international databases available for students. The university administration assigns great importance to the development of academic mobility of the lecturers and works actively on inviting Russian and foreign explorers, well-known in the scientific and educational environment. The university offers different areas of training in specialized Bachelor’s and Master’s programs, as well as other Postgraduate courses.

MEDICAL DEPARTMENT

The medicine course at Chechen State University aims to provide leadership in healthcare with particular emphasis on basic scientific research that underlines medicine. It is a full-time course and allows you to study Medicine, Pediatrics and Dentistry. Inspirational teaching and training, outstanding basic and clinical research will improve medical practice for individuals as well as for population.

The medical institute is located on new Chechen State University campus.

ACCOMMODATION:

Accommodation of a student is one of the most important factor when it comes to living abroad. Russia provides its students with a very good facilities for accommodation. All foreign students stay in student hostels in rooms for 2-3 people. Rooms are provided with the necessary furniture and bedding. 

In winter, all rooms in university hostels are Centrally heated. The average temperature indoors is +25 degree Celsius. Student hostel has a gym, classrooms for studying, laundries, kitchens, and living room for all the students to gather together, well maintained toilets and bathrooms with showers of  hot and cold water supply.

Unlike many other European countries, there is a kitchen on sharing basis for several rooms, where you can prepare meals according to your individual taste and wishes. A broad variety of food products from all over the world that you find in food stores and supermarkets gives foreign citizens an excellent opportunity to stick to the same foods that they were accustomed to.
