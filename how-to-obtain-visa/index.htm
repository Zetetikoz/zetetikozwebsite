It is common knowledge that it is impossible to study in any university abroad without a relevant visa. We understand that it is quite difficult to obtain visa, and in purpose to solve this problem we would like to offer our students the assistance of our Indian Partner office. They are geared towards helping you submit the right documents to ensure that there is no inadvertent delay or rejection. We will assign you a Visa Consultant who will provide comprehensive advice relating to the collation of the relevant documentation that you need to submit along with the visa application. 

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR STUDY VISA IN UKRAINE: 
  • Original International Passport (minimum valid for one year);
  • Original Invitation Letter from the university;
  • Secondary School Certificate;
  • Birth Certificate;
  • Medical Fitness Certificate;
  • Bank statement;
  • Medical insurance;
  • 4 photos, size 3.5 x 4.5.

* If the applicant is under 18 years of age, his/her parents or legal guardian must submit an Affidavit at the notary office that they do not object to his/her studying in Ukraine. 

 * Translation of documents, Apostille Seals, Medical Insurance are provided by us

 

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED TO GET STUDY VISA OF RUSSIAN FEDERATION:
  • International Passport (Traveling Document valid not less than two year);
  • Original School Certificates;
  • The HIV-negative certificate issued not earlier than 3 month before entering the territory of Russian Federation, if the period of stay is more than 90 days;
  • One passport size photo (35×45 mm) with light background.

 

Interview for our Indian applicants is not required!!!

Exception. Candidates who should attend visa Interview are:

  • Those who are already 23 years old and above;
  • Citizen of Nepal, Bangladesh and Shri Lanka.

Our Indian Office Representative will also guide you through the key aspects of interview (if the interview will be required) that you may have with the Embassy.
