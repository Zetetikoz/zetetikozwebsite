PG

Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities are well known all over the world. Lot of medical research institutes are working under Russian & Ukrainian medical Universities. Thousands of well known Scientists, Professors and Academicians have studies and worked at Russian & Ukrainian State Universities. They gave a great input to world of medical sciences.

PG IN MEDICINE/ CLINICAL RESIDENCY 

Laboratories and research institutes of Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities and Academies are well equipped with all modern facilities. All postgraduate students have complete rights to use all facilities available in the Russian & Ukrainian Universities.

At the faculties of Postgraduate Education at Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities or Academies, Postgraduate or Clinical Residency programs are offered in more than 100 medical specialties. Internship, Clinical Residency and Ph.D or Masters programs are offered in all Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities.

Duration of Postgraduate or Clinical Residency programs are:
Surgical : 3 years
Non Surgical: 2 years

On completion of Ph.D or Masters, student has to present the thesis to the commission from Ministry of Health of Russian Federation.

All Postgraduate programs at Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities are taught in English and in Russian Languages according to the programs and curricula approved by the Ministry of Health and Ministry of Education of Russian Federation.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

All students need to complete 1-year preparatory department before starting their main course in order to do nostrification of Bachelor’s and Mater’s Degree

The main objective of the postgraduate training is to allow the students of medical institutions to continue their education and general practitioners to improve their skills for further certification and faculty is directed at the training and improvement of qualification and skills of specialists to master not only up-to date medical information but new modern technologies of today and tomorrow.

The Medical postgraduate faculties of Russian & Ukrainian State Medical Universities and Academies, together with their teaching hospitals and other health organizations in Russia and abroad, will provide the necessary physical and human resources to create a scholarly environment appropriate for the training of medical graduates so that they acquire the knowledge, skills and attitudes to become accomplished physicians and surgeons with the highest moral and ethical standards.  

PH.D AND MASTERS PROGRAMS IN ALL FIELDS.

The students wishing to continue their education at Post Graduate/Master’s or PhD level can contact anytime around the year i.e. without awaiting major intake of September. There is no time restrictions for PG or Ph.D programs. 

Duration Masters: 2 years
PhD: 3 years

The students can continue their PG/ Master’s or PhD level in all programs like Medicine, Engineering, Aviation, Economics, Management, Business Administration, International Technologies, Computer Sciences, Civil Engineering, Arts and Culture and lot of others.   

The study is conducted in English or Russian/Ukrainian language, depends on course and student’s choice. 

REQUIREMENTS:

If you want to apply NOW

Kindly forward us high quality clear scanned copies of following documents.

  •  Filled Application Form (with valid permanent/ current postal address and telephone numbers);
    •  International Passport, the page with picture and data on it (travelling document);
    •  Educational Documents available ( Certificate of O level/ A level / SSCE/ HSSCE/ Bachelor/ Master’s ).

Training in the Ph.D takes place on the premises of the best laboratories and research institutes.

As far as recognition of the PG Degree awarded by the medical universities of Russia/Ukraine, the same is recognized and accepted as equivalent in most countries of Europe by virtue of Russia being a signatory to the Bologna convention. The degrees are also valued in the USA, UK, India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Africa, Middle East, Saudi Arabia, Mauritius and Seychelles where our students are employed.
